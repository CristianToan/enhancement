Customer’s problem statements

1. “I Want to Speak Fluently At Work!”
Many professionals struggle to express themselves fluently in important situations like presentations and business meetings.

Our Solution:
We provide personalized English for Specific Purposes (ESP) training, designed to improve fluency for work purposes, specifically for Vietnamese professionals. Our lessons focus on real-world business communication, such as public speaking, negotiating, presenting graphs and small talk.

2. “I Want to Find Industry-Specific English Training!”
Most English courses are too generic and don't focus on the specific vocabulary or communication styles needed for industries like finance, IT, or marketing.

Our Solution:
We offer industry-specific English courses tailored to your field. Our expert instructors work with you to build work-place vocabulary and speaking confidence, ensuring that you’re prepared for the communication demands of your profession. With our intermediate to advanced English training, you'll gain the skills needed to excel in your industry.
